<p>This reward programme is designed by CCE dept to “catch” pupils showing
random acts of kindness and performing good deeds in line with the School
Values-PRRICE.</p>
<p>Staff, PSG and Office Staff spot pupils doing anything GOOD and give them
the acknowledgement slip and a portion of it goes into a box and&nbsp;three
winners’ names are drawn&nbsp;weekly&nbsp;and more prizes are given out
to these pupils to further reinforce the message that the school promotes,
recognizes and rewards good behaviour.</p>
<p>Some examples of good deeds pupils performed are…..</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Picking up litter on their own</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Helping staff and peers out of kindness</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sharing and caring for peers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Helping teachers, school cleaners &amp; canteen vendors.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Returning any item or money found around the school</p>
</li>
</ul>
