<h4><strong>Why Cyberwellness?</strong></h4>
<p>With increased connectivity, our pupils are exposed to both the “good”
and the “bad” in cyberspace Singapore is one of the most wired cities in
the world and cyberwellness programmes need to start early. Cyberwellness
programme is designed to create awareness and promote safety when exploring
cyber-space.</p>

<h4><strong>Cyber-Wellness Star Awards (CWSAP)</strong></h4>
<p>Fengshan was one of 11 schools shortlisted for the inaugural Cyber-Wellness
Star Awards (CWSAP). Our cyber -wellness ambassadors from Primary 5, Bo
Wen and Ernie designed a poster that showcased the school’s cyber -wellness
programme. Amidst intense competition, our ambassadors did us proud and
it was a valuable learning experience for everyone involved.</p>
