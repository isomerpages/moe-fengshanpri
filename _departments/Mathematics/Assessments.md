<p>Assessment is an important aspect of teaching and learning. It should
be used to support the holistic development of our pupils.</p>
<p>Assessment is to provide information on the learning progress of the child.
In Fengshan, a range of assessment tools, including written and performance
tasks are used to provide feedback to pupils on their mastery of concepts
and skills as well as their strengths and areas for improvement.</p>
