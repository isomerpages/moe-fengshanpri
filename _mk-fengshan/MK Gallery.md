<h2>Racial Harmony Day - 17th July 2023</h2>
<p>MK@Fengshan Celebrated Racial harmony day through many fun and engaging
activities prepared by our dear teachers</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MOE%20MK%20Files/Racial%20Harmony%20Day%2017%20July%2023/moe%20mk%20update%2029%20sept%2023%20picture%201.png">

<p>Chinese calligraphy inspired activity where they traced the words, ‘Respect,&nbsp;
Care, Together and Harmony’ in chinese characters.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/MOE%20MK%20Files/Racial%20Harmony%20Day%2017%20July%2023/moe%20mk%20update%2029%20sept%2023%20picture%202.png">

Kulit.’ They decorated their characters with beautiful designs carried
out puppetry activityes during their learning centre time.</p>

<p>Acrylic keychain making activity where the children designed and baked

as a symbol of respect between friends of different ethnicities.</p>
